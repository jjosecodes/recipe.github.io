Spaghetti

A relaxing and tasty spaghetti with tomato sauce

invented by yours truly

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of spaghetti
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of chopped garlic
  • 1/4 cup of chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of chopped basil
  • 1/4 cup of chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup of chopped oregano
  • 1/4 cup of chopped thyme
  • 1/4 cup of chopped rosemary
  • 1/4 cup of chopped sage
  • 1/4 cup of chopped chives
  • 1/4 cup of chopped mint
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup of chopped parsley

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot
  2. Once the water is boiling, add the spaghetti and cook until al dente
  3. While the spaghetti is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  4. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant
  5. Add the tomatoes, basil, parsley, oregano, thyme, rosemary, sage, chives, mint, and cilantro to the skillet
  6. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the herbs are fragrant
  7. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the skillet
  8. Toss the spaghetti with the tomato sauce until well coated
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  10. Serve the spaghetti hot, garnished with parsley
